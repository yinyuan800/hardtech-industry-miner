## D. Stop/continue decision

End deeper notes with:

| Decision | Meaning | Required next evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Continue | Worth deeper diligence now | named customer, reproducible benchmark, cost model |
| Watch | Strong technical signal but commercial unclear | code, deployment proof, team/company formation |
| Component | Useful but weak standalone company | integration into broader product |
| Stop | Not enough moat or buyer urgency | only revisit after new evidence |
